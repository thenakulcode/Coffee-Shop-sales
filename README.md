# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis | Excel Data Analytics Dashboard

This project represents a complete business-focused Excel Data Analytics project designed to analyze coffee shop sales performance and customer purchasing behavior. The project demonstrates how raw transactional data can be transformed into actionable business insights through data cleaning, KPI tracking, dashboard development, and visualization.

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and uncover patterns that help improve operational efficiency, product strategy, and customer experience.

✅ **Data Cleaning & Transformation (Excel):** Prepared and structured raw sales data for analysis.

✅ **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Identified trends in customer footfall, sales performance, product demand, and ordering behavior.

✅ **Dashboard Development (Excel):** Built an interactive dashboard using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, Conditional Formatting, and Slicers.

✅ **Business Insights Generation:** Analyzed store performance, peak sales hours, product categories, and customer preferences.

✅ **Decision Support Reporting:** Translated analytical findings into actionable business recommendations.

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Peak Business Hours

* The highest quantity of orders occurs during morning hours between 8 AM and 10 AM.
* Customer activity gradually decreases after midday and significantly drops during late evening hours.

### 2. Product Category Performance

* Coffee contributes the largest percentage of overall sales.
* Tea and Bakery products are among the strongest revenue-generating categories.

### 3. Store Location Analysis

* Hell's Kitchen records the highest total revenue.
* Astoria and Lower Manhattan show comparable performance levels.

### 4. Top Revenue-Generating Products

* Barista Espresso leads product sales revenue.
* Brewed Chai Tea, Hot Chocolate, and Gourmet Brewed Coffee are among the top-performing products.

### 5. Customer Preferences

* Regular-sized beverages account for the highest proportion of orders.
* Large-sized beverages closely follow in popularity.

### 6. Weekly Sales Trends

* Sales remain relatively stable across all weekdays.
* No significant decline is observed between weekdays and weekends.
